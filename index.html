Regions routing number is a 9-digit bank code which appears at the bottom left corner of the checks drawn by this bank. This code is used to produce sorting, bundling and shipment of paper checks back to check writer's account. Routing number that is also referred as Routing Transit or ABA number is required for making money transfer and certain online transactions.

Routing Numbers are assigned by American Banker's Association to identify all US Financial Institutions.They are used for clearing negotiable instruments such as checks, processing FedWire funds transfers and making direct deposits and some other bill payments.
